Scooters

Our high quality scooters are made for the terrain of mexico. They offer enoough power, range, and durability to last as long as you need them too for your adventure. Although they are mostly for the paved roads, these scooters are specially designed for any bumpy or rough conditions you may face on the roads. This makes for a great experience when exploring Cozumel!

ATVs

These ATVs are built for fun! With the high-powered engine and offroad tires, there is nothing that can get in the way of your adventure. Take one of these babies out on the jungle roads and you will never want to turn back!
