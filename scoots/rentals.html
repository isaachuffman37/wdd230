Scooters

Honda Metro

This is the standard, single-person scooter with a 49cc engine. It has great gas mileage and is a perfect option for anyone that wants to get around Playcara or Cozumel. Try it out!

Honda Dio

This scooter is a step up in quality, capacity, and speed. The Dio can hold up to 2 people, has a 110cc engine, and still has great fuel economy. If you havae a friend or a loved one that wants to join you on your adventures, this is the scooter for you!

Honda PCX150

This is one bad scooter. When we say bad, we mean perfectly good in every way. This baby has a 149cc engine that can take you anywhere you want to go. It also has a capacity for 2 people and can handle any terrain you encounter out there on the road to adventure!

ATVs

Honda Pioneer 1000

If you like to tear it up on the trails with your friends, this is the vehicle you need. There are many forest/jungle trails around Cozumel and Playacara, and this beast can handle them all. If you don't mind having all the freedom in the world, we would recommend getting this off-road vehicle.

Jeeps

4-Door

This is the apex of human innovation. This will allow you to be the king of any road you travel on. With ice cold A/C and 5 seats, there is nothing that will stop you other than your own limitations.

2-Door

This vehicle is okay. Not the best, not the worst. If you are going to spend money on a jeep you might as well get the one with A/C. We won't judge you if you get this one though... much.
